Brazillian Style Collard Greens (Couve)


Ingredients:

  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp pepper (light or regular)
  • 1 lb collard greens
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions:

Preparation:

Chop up the garlic clove.

Rinse the greens well with cold water.

Pile several leaves on top of each other. Roll the leaves up together length wise.

With a sharp knife, slice the roll of leaves into thin strips.

Cooking:

Heat the oil in a large frying pan

Saute the chopped garlic.

Add the greens and saute.

Add salt to taste.

Serve immediately.

Serves 4
