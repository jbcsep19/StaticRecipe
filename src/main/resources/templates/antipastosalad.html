Mishmash Antipasto Salad




From Italy/USA




Ingredients:

Antipasto Salad Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. dry pasta (Tri-color rotini is recommended...)
  • 4 cups chopped vegetable ingredients (red peppers, yellow peppers, green peppers, orange peppers, red and/or white onions)
  • 1 cup of cut/diced tomatoes (Roma and/or Cherry)
  • 1/2 cup of Italian cheese(s) combination (optional)
  • 1 1/3 cups of meats (Genoa salami, Hard salami, Pepperoni, Ham) (optional)


Vinaigrette Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon (each) of 2 different mustards


Directions:


To Make The Antipasto Salad:

1. Cook the pasta in a large pot according to package directions. Adding a pinch (or 2) of Kosher salt is recommended. Drain pasta and rinse under cold water until no longer hot.


2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, chopped vegetables, tomatoes and meats. Drizzle the Vinaigrette on top, then toss to combine. Best served if chilled in refrigerator overnight.


To Make The Vinaigrette:

1. Whisk all ingredients together until combined.



Serves at least 8 people
